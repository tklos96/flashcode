Dean M. Townsley 2009

This subroutine interpolates within a 1-dimensional WD profile by averaging over a cell width. The WD profile, stored in the Simulation Unit's module data area, is averaged over an interval of size "dr" at radius "radius". Returned are the density, temperature, and c12 and ne22 mass fractions. Temperature averaging is just multiplicatively mass-weighted for lack of a better thing to do.

Averaging over an interval is used to allow a graceful mapping onto the unrefined grid during initial refinement.
